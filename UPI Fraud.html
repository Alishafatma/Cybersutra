🏦 What is UPI?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a digital payment system in India that allows you to send or receive money instantly using your mobile phone. You only need a UPI ID (like name@bank) or scan a QR code.

➡️

➡️
3

➡️
4

Money gets deducted

➡️
5

You become a victim of fraud

❌ Warning Signs

  • Unknown payment requests
  • Urgency / pressure
  • Asking for PIN / OTP
  • Fake customer care calls

✅ Safety Tips

  • UPI PIN is only for sending money
  • Never approve unknown requests
  • Never share your PIN / OTP
  • Always verify before payment
