📘 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a scam where attackers create fake websites, emails or messages that look real to steal your information.

⚠️ How Phishing Works

You get fake message

➡️

Looks like real site

➡️

You enter details

➡️

Data stolen ❌

❌ Fake

  • Strange URL
  • Spelling mistakes
  • Urgent message
  • Unknown sender

✅ Real

  • Official domain
  • No pressure
  • Verified sender
  • Secure connection 🔒

📌 Real Life Examples

  • "Your bank account will be blocked! Click here"
  • "You won ₹10,000 reward – claim now"
  • "Update your KYC immediately"
  • "Login link from fake website"
