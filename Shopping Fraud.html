Online Shopping Fraud

📘 What is Shopping Fraud?

Fake websites or sellers trick users with huge discounts and steal money.

⚠️ How It Works

  • Fake website shows big discounts
  • You pay online
  • Product never arrives

🚨 Warning Signs

  • Too good to be true offers
  • No reviews
  • Unknown websites
