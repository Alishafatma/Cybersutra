🪪 KYC Awareness

Know Your Customer • Stay Safe

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a process where banks or apps verify your identity. It helps prevent fraud and keeps your account secure.

📘 What is KYC?

KYC means confirming who you are using valid documents like Aadhaar, PAN card, or mobile verification. It is required for bank accounts, UPI apps, wallets, etc.

⚠️ KYC Scam (Important)

Scammers send fake messages or call you saying your KYC is expired. They try to steal your OTP, bank details or money.

  • "Your KYC expired – update now!"
  • "Click link to avoid account block"
  • "Share OTP to complete KYC"

❌ What NOT to Do

  • Never share OTP or PIN
  • Do not click unknown links
  • Do not trust random calls
  • Do not install unknown apps

✅ What to Do

  • Use official bank app/website
  • Check URL before entering details
  • Visit bank branch if unsure
  • Verify messages before action

📌 Real Life Examples

  • "Update your KYC now or account will be blocked"
  • "Download app for KYC update"
  • "Send OTP to complete verification"
