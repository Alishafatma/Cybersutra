🔐 OTP Scam Awareness

Never Share Your OTP

🛡 OTP protects your account from unauthorized access

⚠ Scammers try to trick you into revealing OTP

💡 One small mistake can lead to big financial loss

📘 What is OTP?

OTP (One Time Password) is a temporary secure code sent to your mobile number to verify your identity during login or transactions.

Secure Login

Transaction Verification

Account Protection

⚠️ How OTP Scam Happens

1

Scammer contacts you (call/message)

➡️
2

They create urgency or fake reason

➡️
3

You share OTP unknowingly

➡️
4

Your account is accessed / money stolen

❌ Warning Signs

  • Unknown calls asking OTP
  • Urgent / threatening tone
  • Fake bank messages
  • Prize / lottery scams

✅ Safety Tips

  • Never share OTP with anyone
  • Bank never asks OTP
  • Always verify before acting
  • Block suspicious numbers

💡 Stay alert, stay safe!
