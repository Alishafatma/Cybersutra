🏦 Bank Scam Awareness

Protect Your Money • Stay Alert

Bank scams happen when fraudsters pretend to be bank officials and try to steal your personal or financial information.

📘 What is Bank Scam?

A bank scam is a fraud where attackers trick users into sharing sensitive details like OTP, ATM PIN, or bank info.

⚠️ How Bank Scam Happens

Scammer calls you
➡️
Pretends to be bank
➡️
Asks details (OTP/PIN)
➡️
You share info
➡️
Money stolen ❌

❌ Warning Signs

  • Unknown calls from "bank"
  • Urgent request ("account blocked")
  • Asking OTP / PIN
  • Suspicious links

✅ Safety Tips

  • Bank never asks OTP
  • Never share PIN
  • Ignore unknown calls
  • Use official apps only
